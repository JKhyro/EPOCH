# Education Operating Platform Boundary

## Status

WORKSHOP-owned extraction candidate.

This document remains in EPOCH only to preserve historical prototype context
until the education operating platform is fully moved into WORKSHOP.

## Correct ownership

WORKSHOP owns the education operating platform as a revenue/service product:

- premium education packages
- EIKEN 5 through 1, TOEIC, IELTS, TOEFL, school/university writing, business
  English, and professional communication tracks
- paid diagnostics
- submission review packs
- cohort labs
- premium exam sprints
- subscription study-material and strategy access
- pricing, offer copy, lead conversion, compatibility assessment, and
  under-19 acceptance rules

EPOCH owns only the schedule-bound support layer for those workflows:

- class/session calendar entries
- cohort schedules
- submission and review deadlines
- follow-up checkpoints
- schedule-safe student/customer status events
- reminder and notification timing

## Positioning rules retained for WORKSHOP

- Lead Japan-facing offers with outcomes, structure, teacher-reviewed guidance,
  scheduling reliability, progress tracking, and professional support.
- Do not lead with AI terminology in English-education marketing.
- Do not sell generic tutoring as the primary product; sell exam/result
  coaching, structured writing review, cohort labs, submission packs, and
  managed progress.
- Prefer adults and serious students. Students under 19 should require
  compatibility assessment, guardian agreement, and either a limited accepted
  track or higher-touch pricing.
- Keep 1:1 live teaching premium and scarce. Use cohorts, submissions, and
  asynchronous review to reduce labor.

## Integration rule

WORKSHOP sends schedule-bound work to EPOCH. EPOCH returns schedule state and
deadline/status events. WORKSHOP remains responsible for revenue, pedagogy,
service delivery, customer operations, and marketing.
